Title Do not give IPTp-SP, because woman is taking co-trimoxazole
Description Do not give IPTp-SP, because woman is taking co-trimoxazole
Human-interpretable Description IF the woman is currently taking co-trimoxazole, even in a malaria-endemic setting, she should not receive any dose of IPTp-SP. Women who are taking co-trimoxazole SHOULD NOT receive IPTp-SP. Taking both co-trimoxazole and IPTp-SP together can enhance side-effects, especially haematological side-effects such as anaemia.
