<tr>


<th scope="row" class="row-header">Human-interpretable Description</th>


<td class="content-container">In populations with low dietary calcium intake, daily calcium supplementation (1.5–2.0 g oral elemental calcium) is recommended for pregnant women to reduce the risk of pre-eclampsia. 

Dietary counselling of pregnant women should promote adequate calcium intake through locally available, calcium-rich foods.

– Divide the total dose into three doses, preferably taken at meal-time.
– Iron and calcium should preferably be administered several hours apart rather than concomitantly.

Please refer to calcium sources listed below for additional guidance that can be provided. </td>
</tr>
